About the Virtual Ruck

The Virtual Ruck allows participants from around the world to honor our fallen heroes from wherever they are. Whether you're unable to attend an in-person event due to distance, schedule conflicts, or other commitments, the Virtual Ruck ensures everyone can participate in remembering and honoring those who made the ultimate sacrifice.

Complete your ruck march at your own pace, in your own location. Carry a weighted pack as you walk or run the designated distance, reflecting on the burden carried by our service members. Share your experience on social media using our event hashtag, connect with fellow participants virtually, and be part of a global community united in remembrance.
