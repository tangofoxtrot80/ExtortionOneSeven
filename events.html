Memorial Events

Join Us in Remembrance

We invite you to join us for a memorial hike honoring the 31 brave service members who made the ultimate sacrifice in service to our nation. This annual gathering brings together families, friends, fellow veterans, and community members to remember, reflect, and pay tribute to those who gave their lives defending our freedoms.

The main events (DC and Florida) occur overnight, when most of these men operated. Virtual events occur wherever and whenever you choose.

Here's a general gear list of what you should bring with you:

  • A Ruck (Military slang for a backpack)
  • Water bladder or container to hold water.
  • Any food/snacks you may want.
  • A change of socks.
  • A poncho in case it rains.
  • A light source and spare batteries. Think flashlight, headlamp, etc.
  • Reflective gear
  • Reception to follow

All are welcome to attend this free public event. Military and veterans are encouraged to wear their uniforms. We ask that attendees dress respectfully for this solemn occasion.
