Memorial Events

Join Us in Remembrance

We invite you to join us for a solemn memorial ceremony honoring the 31 brave service members who made the ultimate sacrifice in service to our nation. This annual gathering brings together families, friends, fellow veterans, and community members to remember, reflect, and pay tribute to those who gave their lives defending our freedoms.

The memorial event will feature military honors, keynote speakers, a candlelight vigil, and opportunities to meet with Gold Star families. This is a time to come together as a community to ensure that the sacrifices of these heroes are never forgotten and that their legacies continue to inspire future generations.

The ceremony will include:

  • Opening remarks and invocation
  • Military honor guard presentation
  • Reading of the names
  • Guest speakers and remembrances
  • Wreath laying ceremony
  • Moment of silence
  • Closing benediction
  • Reception to follow

All are welcome to attend this free public event. Military and veterans are encouraged to wear their uniforms. We ask that attendees dress respectfully for this solemn occasion.
